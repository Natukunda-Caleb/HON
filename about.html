Who is Kamukama Davis ?

Hon Kamukama Davis Araali, A mutooro by tribe, born in July 1987 from Bunyangabu District Kibiito town-council.

Joined politics in way back in time while he was in highschool at kabarole hill side highschool as the headprefect.

He laiter went to South Africa for his degree in horticulture at Free State University.

He is a business man dealing in general merchandise operating from Kikubo a Kampala business center.

He laiter joined active politics in Bunyangabu district in 2018 and contested on the Bunyangabu County Parliamentary seat.

And won by then minister of defense and veteran affairs Hon Adolf Mwesige who is now the clerk to Parliament.

Hon Kamukama Davis is a hard working energetic youth , who fully focused and ready to continue serving his people of Bunyangabu district and the entire nation.
